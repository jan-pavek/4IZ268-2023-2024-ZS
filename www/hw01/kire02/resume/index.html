Doja Cat

Amala Ratna Zandile Dlamini (Zulu: ['zandile 'ɮamini]; born October 21, 1995), known professionally as Doja Cat (/'doʊdʒə/ DOH-jə), is an American rapper and singer. Born and raised in Los Angeles, she began making and releasing music on SoundCloud as a teenager.
